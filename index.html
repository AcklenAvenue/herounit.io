Talent and Passion in Unexpected Places

Byron Sommardahl, the founder of HeroUnit, has lived with one foot in Nashville, TN and another foot in Honduras since 2010 when he, as his pastor says, "went on a mission trip and never came back." After meeting the girl of his dreams in Honduras, and beginning a life between two countries, Byron and his partners at Acklen Avenue Software discovered immense and mostly unnoticed technical talent in Latin America and especially in Honduras! That talent is what drives both Acklen Avenue and HeroUnit today.

Did you know that Honduras and Nashville are in the same time-zone? Did you know that Honduras is about 4 hours away by plane? Did you realize that the people of Honduras are hungry to show the world what they are made of? We are thriving on those facts and have since 2010!

HeroUnit provides just-in-time technical talent in the areas of mobile, web, back-end, and devops. How can we save your world?
