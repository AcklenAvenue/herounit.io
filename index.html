Why HeroUnit?

(Basically, not everyone else can be a hero)

Team-work philosophy

We work as a team. With very few exceptions, there are always at least two heroes working together to solve your problems, this way we can share knowledge and prevent silos, and also keep our heroes focused and accountable.

Emergency (Proactive) Support

While your team handles customer-level support, we’ll be ready to handle the toughest engineering-level issues. When you’re in a jam, HeroUnit is ready just in time.

Credit-based maintenance

Your team will follow your lead, spending credits from your bucket each month. It’s up to you what they work on and when. One credit is the same as one hour if the team uses it during normal USA business hours. But you have the flexibility to use credits at other times as well.

Complementary Services

In addition to the work you decide the team will perform, the HeroUnit team will also take it upon themselves to ensure your application is constantly improving using various tools and methods depending on your level of support.
